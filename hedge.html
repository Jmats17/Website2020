HEDGE
Hedge Sports is the first daily fantasy sports platform to utilize spare change round-ups in contests. Bought a coffee for $2.50? The other 50 cents get rounded up into a contest where you pick from a pool of players for contest-specific games and enter them for your chance at cash prizes against other users. View on App Store
