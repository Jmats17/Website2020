Egghunt
Egghunt is an augmented reality, location-based scavenger hunt that create a fun competitive mission amongst the participants involved. Users find Egghunts in their area and compete to discover unravel the clues that lead to eggs in their area. Companies can use Egghunt to sponsor hunts to support their brand in the area and have users compete for their grand prize in order to promote their product and service.
