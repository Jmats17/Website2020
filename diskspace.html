Disk Space
Disk Space serves as a standalone storage widget app that lives in the widget screen on your device. Seen as a problem to myself and others who had difficulty remembering where the storage was located, Disk Space was created to easily and quickly located the free and total space you have left on your device by simply sliding to the widgets screen and viewing.
