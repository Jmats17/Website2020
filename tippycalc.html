TippyCalc Udemy Tutorial
An in-progress tip calculator app tutorial being developed to teach the basics of Swift app development. The point of the tutorial is to teach those interested in app development how to navigate XCode and Storyboard, the basics of Swift such as variables and functions and producing results to display.
